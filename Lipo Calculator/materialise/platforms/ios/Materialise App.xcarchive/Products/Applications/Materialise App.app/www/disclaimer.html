Disclaimer

The Lipocalculator tool is used to communicate information about the proposed surgical treatment for liposuction. It is not a substitute for the consent or the guidance of the individual physician. The calculation process attempts to define principles liposuction safety that should generally meet the needs of most patients in most circumstances.

However, the calculation tool should not be considered all inclusive or perfect in defining all methods of care and risks encountered. Individual surgeons must provide and consider all additional or different information which is based on all the facts in your particular case and the current state of medical knowledge.

The calculations are not intended to define or serve as the standard of medical care. Standards of medical care are determined on the basis of all of the facts involved in an individual case and are subject to change as scientific knowledge and technology advance and as practice patterns evolve.
